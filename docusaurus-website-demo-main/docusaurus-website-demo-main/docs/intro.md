# Introduction to Agentic AI Systems

Welcome to the comprehensive documentation for Agentic AI Systems Research Project. This documentation provides an in-depth exploration of agentic AI concepts, from foundational principles to advanced implementation patterns.

## What are Agentic AI Systems?

Agentic AI systems are artificial intelligence systems that exhibit goal-directed behavior, making autonomous decisions to achieve specific objectives. These systems are characterized by their ability to:

- Plan and execute complex sequences of actions
- Use tools and interact with external systems
- Learn from experience and adapt their behavior
- Operate with minimal human intervention
